Introduction

Reimagining LinkedIn’s user experience to enhance engagement, personalization, and usability

As part of a UX research and design project, I explored ways to enhance LinkedIn’s interface by addressing user frustrations with notifications, content personalization, and job search tools. My goal was to streamline navigation, improve content relevance, and make job searching more intuitive for users at different career stages.

  • Conducted user research and competitive analysis
  • Developed UX strategy and design principles
  • wireframes and prototypes to visualize solutions

    • The Problem

  • LinkedIn is a crucial platform for professionals, yet many users experience challenges that reduce engagement
  • Notification Overload: Users receive excessive notifications, leading to frustration and disengagement.
  • Cluttered & Irrelevant Content: The home feed lacks personalization, making it difficult to find valuable updates.
  • Inefficient Job Search: The job filtering system does not cater well to entry-level professionals or location-based preferences.
  • Through research, I identified key improvements to make LinkedIn more intuitive, engaging, and user-friendly. yles and mediums to create meaningful, visually compelling work.

    • Research & Insights,

    User Interviews: Spoke with 5 LinkedIn users across different career stages to understand pain points

    Competitive Analysis: Studied job-search and professional networking platforms to identify best practices.

    1. Notification Fatigue

  • feel overwhelmed by frequent and irrelevant notifications.
  • get too many notifications, and most of them aren’t useful.
  • Customizable notification settings & AI-powered filtering.

    • 2. Content Overload & Lack of Relevance

  • struggle to find useful posts among ads and low-value content.
  • feed is flooded with posts I don’t care about. I wish I could filter it better.
  • Solution: Feed segmentation & interest-based content recommendations

    • 3. Ineffective Job Search for Entry-Level Users

  • job seekers struggle to find relevant opportunities due to poor filtering options.
  • it’s hard to find jobs that match my experience level and location.
  • Dedicated “0 Years Experience” filter & improved location-based search.

    • The Problem

    LinkedIn should empower users with intuitive tools that enhance engagement, improve job search efficiency, and provide relevant, personalized content. Our redesign focuses on streamlined notifications, tailored content, and simplified job search to create a more user-centric experience."

    Final Design & Solutions,

    Key Features

    1. Redesigned Notification System

  • Custom notification preferences for job seekers, recruiters, and professionals.
  • AI-driven priority notifications for relevant updates. .
  • “Do Not Disturb” mode for better work-life balance.

    • 2. Personalized & Streamlined Feed

  • Users can switch between different feed categories for targeted browsing.
  • Intelligent content curation ensures relevant updates.
  • Mute/unfollow options for better content control.

    • 3. Enhanced Job Search Experience

  • “0 Years Experience” filter helps entry-level job seekers find suitable roles.
  • Advanced filtering for job location, remote work, and salary preferences.
  • Smart job recommendations powered by AI.
