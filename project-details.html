Introduction

Reimagining LinkedIn’s user experience to enhance engagement, personalization, and usability

As part of a UX research and design project, I explored ways to enhance LinkedIn’s interface by addressing user frustrations with notifications, content personalization, and job search tools. My goal was to streamline navigation, improve content relevance, and make job searching more intuitive for users at different career stages.

My Role:
  • Conducted user research and competitive analysis
  • Developed UX strategy and design principles
  • Created wireframes
  • ualize solutiand prototypes to visons

The Problem

• LinkedIn is a crucial platform for professionals, yet many users experience challenges that reduce engagement: • Notification Overload: Users receive excessive notifications, leading to frustration and disengagement. • Cluttered & Irrelevant Content: The home feed lacks personalization, making it difficult to find valuable updates. • Inefficient Job Search: The job filtering system does not cater well to entry-level professionals or location-based preferences. • Through research, I identified key improvements to make LinkedIn more intuitive, engaging, and user-friendly.

Research & Insights

Research Methods • User Interviews: Spoke with 5 LinkedIn users across different career stages to understand pain points.• Competitive Analysis: Studied job-search and professional networking platforms to identify best practices.

    Notification Fatigue
  • • Users feel overwhelmed by frequent and irrelevant notifications.
  • • get too many notifications, and most of them aren’t useful.
  • • Solution: Customizable notification settings & AI-powered filtering.
    • Content Overload & Lack of Relevance
  • • Users struggle to find useful posts among ads and low-value content.
  • • My feed is flooded with posts I don’t care about. I wish I could filter it better.
  • • Solution: Feed segmentation & interest-based content recommendations.

Strategy & Design Principles

LinkedIn should empower users with intuitive tools that enhance engagement, improve job search efficiency, and provide relevant, personalized content. Our redesign focuses on streamlined notifications, tailored content, and simplified job search to create a more user-centric experience.

Design Principles & Recommendations 1️-Streamlined & Personalized Notifications - “Customizable notification settings” (type, frequency, delivery method). - “AI-powered filtering” to highlight relevant updates. - “Do Not Disturb (DND) Mode” for focused work time. 2️-Context-Driven Content Personalization - Feed Segmentation: Users can toggle between “Jobs,” “Industry News,” and “Network Updates.” - Field-Specific Content Recommendations: based on career interests. - Ability to **hide or mute** irrelevant posts and ads. 3️- Simplified Job Search & Filtering Tools Dedicated “0 Years Experience” filter** for new graduates. - Enhanced location-based filtering for better job discovery. - Smart recommendations based on search history & user preferences.
