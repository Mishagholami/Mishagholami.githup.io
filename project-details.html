Research

For my research, I chose LinkedIn because it's a powerful platform that blends job searching, networking, and industry insights all in one place. Unlike other social media, LinkedIn is career-focused, helping people find opportunities, connect with professionals, and stay informed on industry trends.

User Research

Through comprehensive user research, I identified key pain points and opportunities for improvement in the LinkedIn experience. This involved analyzing user behavior, conducting surveys, and gathering feedback from various professionals.

Design Process

The design process involved creating wireframes, prototypes, and iterative designs based on user feedback. Each iteration brought improvements to the user interface and overall experience.

Visual Design

The visual design phase focused on creating a clean, professional interface that aligns with LinkedIn's brand while improving usability and accessibility for all users.

Final Results

The final design successfully addressed user needs while maintaining LinkedIn's professional aesthetic. The improvements resulted in a more intuitive and engaging platform for professional networking.
